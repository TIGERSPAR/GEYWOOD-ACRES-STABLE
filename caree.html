How to Take Care of Your horse

  • 1. Nutrition: Feed high-quality puppy food 3–4 times a day.
  • 2. Exercise: Provide daily playtime and short walks.
  • 3. Training: Start with basic commands like sit, stay, and come.
  • 4. Health: Visit the vet regularly for checkups and vaccinations.
  • 5. Grooming: Brush your puppy’s coat and bathe them as needed.
  • 6. Socialization: Expose your puppy to other pets and people early on.
